Super Marcos

🎮 Advanced Controls:

Arrow Keys: Move | Spacebar: Jump | Shift: Dash

Double Jump: Press Spacebar twice in air

Wall Jump: Slide on wall + Spacebar

🎯 Game Features:

Collect coins (gold) and power-ups (colored boxes)

Defeat enemies by jumping on them

Reach the golden goal to complete levels!

⌨️ Menu Controls:

P: Pause | R: Restart | ESC: Menu | ENTER: Start
